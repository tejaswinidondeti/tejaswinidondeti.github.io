About Me

I'm a results-driven GRC Analyst with hands-on experience across risk assessments, compliance audits, privacy programs, and security framework implementations. I've worked with organizations spanning SaaS, banking, healthcare, defense, critical infrastructure, and AI — helping them achieve and maintain compliance while reducing risk.

My expertise covers 15+ frameworks including ISO 27001, NIST CSF, SOC 2, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, CCPA, CMMC, CIS Controls, COBIT, and emerging standards like ISO 42001 and the EU AI Act. I specialize in cross-framework harmonization and building scalable GRC programs.
